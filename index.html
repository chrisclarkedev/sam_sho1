What is Samurai Showdown?

A competetive fighting game developed by SNK for their Neo Geo arcade and home platform, released in 1993 being the first installment in the long running franchise. In a period when there were fighting games based on modern hand to hand combat. The "Samurai Showdown" franchise was a stark contrast to what was out at the time. Set in 18th century feudal-era japan this fighter focused on weapon-based combat.

More info on the Samurai Showdown Wikia

Plot

Amakusa Shiro slain in Japan (1638) by the Tokugawa government for his part in the "Shimbara tax revolt". By 1787 Shiro gets resurrected as a demon after making a deal with the dark lord Ambrosia. Driven by hatred for the Tokugawa Shogunate he unleashed his dark powers to bring chaos to all existence.

A variety of warriors some being historic, some fictional converge upon the source of chaos that Amakusa Shiro has brought into the world, each driven solely for their own reasons.

Gameplay

"Focus your mind, draw your blade"

Renowed for its fast pace. With a roster of 12 characters, each character specializing in a different weapon and fighting style. Gameplay focuses primarily on quick powerful strikes than combos. Emphasizing slow motion when a player gets hit with a strong attack to intensify damage delt.

During the match a referee holds flags representing each player (white player 1; red player 2) when respective player get hit he raises the flag. Randomly a delivery man running in the background throws items suchs as bombs (deals damage) or chicken (heals) which changes the outcome of fights.
